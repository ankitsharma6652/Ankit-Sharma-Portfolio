SpendTracker

Privacy-first automatic expense tracker for Android

Tracks your spending with zero manual entry. It reads bank SMS and UPI notifications on-device, detects every payment in real time, and pops a one-tap categorize dialog over any app the moment money leaves your account. No internet permission by design — financial data physically cannot leave the phone. A hand-tuned regex engine (trained on thousands of real Indian bank messages) extracts amount, merchant, and account; self-transfers and credit-card bill payments are auto-detected and excluded from totals; and categorizing one merchant retroactively tags every past payment from it. Try the interactive browser demo — it fires simulated bank notifications so you can experience the whole auto-detection flow without an Android phone.
